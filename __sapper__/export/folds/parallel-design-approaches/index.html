Parallel [Design] Approaches

Parallel [Design] Approaches

3-14

Our issue encourages further exploration, underscoring how the tools of designers from other disciplines can be assembled toward new ends, inspiring dialogues and collaborative relationships between design fields.

With a total of 16 responses from the contributors, we discovered 4 parallel approaches in submitting their responses:

  • Architects: MIcrosoft Word documents, ready for download
  • Tech-Savvy : Shared Google Docs with anonymous accounts
  • Graphic Designers: Typeset/numbered/bullet point forms
  • Artists: Human exchange (interviews)

We hope that this issue allows you to speculate on possibilities and opportunities in design.
