Halloween II

Halloween II

4-05

And so another Halloween season is upon us: the days have become crisp and short, the leaf-less trees now stand skeletal against the pitch black night, children are preparing their demonic masks, and the spirits have begun their ascent from the grave. No All Hallows’ Eve would be complete, however, without the release of the yearly Paprika! Halloween issue! Reader beware. however, because you’re in for a scare: within these ghastly pages is a first-hand account of a paranormal investigation, an exploration of the spectral life of ancient manuscripts, and the tale of a client so evil that Peter Eisenman himself turned down a commission!

Don’t worry, not all is terror and gloom; there are some treats amidst the tricks: 150 lucky readers will find their Paprika! stuffed with a limited edition risograph print of the scariest houses in all of suburbia! Happy Halloween Boils and Ghouls!
