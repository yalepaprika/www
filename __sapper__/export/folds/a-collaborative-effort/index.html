A Collaborative Effort

A Collaborative Effort

1-02

Fold 2 showcases the work produced for the event Lux: Ideas Through Light, a series of projections on the Beinecke Library from April 10th-12th, 2015. Coupled with student work by Daniel Glick-Unterman (M. Arch. 2017) and Luis Salas Porras (M. Arch. 2016), both of whom test the traditional boundaries of two and three-dimensional architectural representations, this fold also features interviews with Keller Easterling and Tatiana Bilbao. The graphic design by Jiyoni Kim delivers a playful interpretation of icons and language of the digital screen brought to print.
