Fiction

3-15

Architecture students make fictional buildings.

Architects make real buildings. Sometimes they make fictional buildings too.

Writers of fiction put real buildings in their stories.

Other times they invent fictional buildings for their stories.

The more we thought about architecture and fiction, the more confused we got.

So we issued an open call to the school asking for works of fiction. Or works about fiction.

This is the fiction issue.
