PAPRIKA! DINNER
Thursday, July 11, 7 30 PM
Rux Studios, Queens
10-40 46th Ave, Long Island City, NY 11101
Guest of Honor:Â  Erik Freer
Co-hosts: Madelynn Ringo, Julie Turgeon, Tess McNamara, Nicolas Kemper

We are happy to announce the completion and launch of Paprika! Volume 1, containing the complete first year of Paprika!, reproduced at full size.

To celebrate the event, we are having a dinner to thank our graphic designer Erik Freer for his work, and raise funds to support Paprika!

Yale School of Architecture alumni Chris Beardsley and Russell Greenberg have offered to host us in their design studio, Rux. Madelynn Ringo and Nicolas Kemper are providing dinner.

Tickets are are by donation. Donations above $90 include a copy of Volume 1 (MSRP $80).


All funds raised will go to support the student operation. An additional $30 will help fund snacks for Paprika meetings, while $530 will provide 500 copies of an entire issue in color.
