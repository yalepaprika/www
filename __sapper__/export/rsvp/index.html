PAPRIKA! TURNS 5
Saturday, September 14, 6PM
7th Floor Terrace, Rudolph Hall
180 York Street, New Haven, CT, 06511

Paprika! is turning 5 years old, and we are excited to celebrate with friends, alumni, and Paprika-lovers! Join us on the Yale School of Architecture’s rooftop terrace to enjoy the sunset with unlimited drinks (including a signature Paprika! cocktail), New Haven pizza, and live music.

The event will feature an art action, where guests can bid to take home framed works by Yale art and architecture students. Paprika! Volume 1 (issues from the first year reprinted) will also be available for purchase at a special reduced price.

Help us ring in our 5th year and raise funds to support Paprika!

Tickets are $40 dollars each and include food and an open bar. Once purchased, your name will be added to the guestlist at the entrance. Additional tickets will be under the purchaser’s name.

All funds raised will go to support the student operation.
