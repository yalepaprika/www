Why should I get a Paprika! print subscription?

Paprika! is an art object. Every issue of Paprika! is a newspaper broadsheet uniquely designed by students from Yale’s Graphic Design program. No two issues are alike.

Paprika! print subscriptions support our 100% student-run publication. Every issue is student-curated and aims to broadcast diverse voices in the fields of art, architecture and design.

Paprika! features content from prominent figures in the profession. Past issues have printed interviews and conversations with:

  • Stan Allen
  • Assemble Studio
  • Iwan Baan
  • Deborah Berke
  • Tatiana Bilbao
  • Keller Easterling
  • Peter Eisenman
  • César Pelli
  • Patrik Schumacher
  • Robert A.M. Stern
  • T+E+A+M
  • Anthony Vidler

What does a Paprika! print subscription look like?

Paprika! prints approximately 18 issues a year from September to April. Each issue is released and mailed on a Thursday. All plans include shipping and are automatically renewed annually. Prices reflect a 1-year subscription.
