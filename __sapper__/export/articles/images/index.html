Illusion, Deception

November 15, 2018

A note on the images:
This spread is a collection of images which highlight a pervasive culture of illusion and deception that we participate in everyday as designers and as citizens. Surrounded by deceptive projections and borders of our environment (A), mediated private platforms (B), bleeding-edge technologies void of ethics (C), manipulative media practices (D), and tools of salesmanship (E), it’s critical for designers to reflect on the consequences of our work.
