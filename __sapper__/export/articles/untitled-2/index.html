Other, Etc. A Catalog Of Anything But Architecture

August 29, 2019

Slowly drift as I elevate
And see beyond temptations that attempt to test my element,
A striking slur of words engulf so sinister yet eloquent,
Elaborately striking as he scribbles down with elegance.
A fleeting fable, viciously teeming life,
And so I stop, contemplate and translate and transcribe,
And present to you with grace,
An invitation to my mind.
— [L]ife x
