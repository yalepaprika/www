ARTISTS TALK : Stephanie Gonzalez-Turner

  • Stephanie Gonzalez-Turner

    M.F.A., 2017

2-04

Work

October 6, 2016

(Painting and Printmaking ‘17)

The ultimate goal is to turn [my artworks] into physical forms that have dimensionality. A lot of it is trial and error, in every step of the process—starting out with a really concrete idea of why this language interests me, and how I want to use it, but very quickly, finding it constantly undoing itself…which is ultimately good, I think. It’s how it moves beyond a thesis.

That the work would transcend the thing that invented it.

Right, process can accomplish that. For people like me, who are figuring out the material they’re working with, there’s so much learning involved in that, and experimentation within it, that there are all of these opportunities for expansion. And I do want it to move forward.
