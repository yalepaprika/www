Frame + Essence

February 23, 2017

I view architecture as a continuation of a long tradition of mediation between man and his environment. The past is as relevant as the future, and limitations are its most immediate canvas. Each instance is an opportunity to redefine a present belief, adapt a foreign substrate into the present context, and assimilate ideas into form & structure, with the goal of creating a unique experience every time. The constant dialogue is what lends it dynamism, and continues this tradition of ever-changing needs, hopes, and aspirations.
